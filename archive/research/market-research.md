# Market Research: Group Coworking Platform (India/HSR Layout)

## 1. Competitor Analysis

### Direct Competitors (Virtual Group Coworking)
**None of these do IRL (in-person) group matching — donedonadone occupies genuine whitespace.**

| Platform | Model | Pricing | Key Feature | Gap for us |
|----------|-------|---------|-------------|------------|
| **Focusmate** | 1-on-1 virtual pairing | Free (3/wk), $6.99-9.99/mo unlimited | Accountability partner matching | Virtual only, no groups, no IRL |
| **Caveday** | Facilitator-led virtual sessions | $30-39/mo | Structured deep work, guided | Virtual only, expensive, no IRL |
| **Flow Club** | Group virtual coworking | $40/mo | Host-led sessions, ADHD-friendly | Virtual only, high price point |
| **FLOWN** | Virtual coworking community | ~$15-25/mo | Deep work focus, nature sounds | Virtual only |

### Indian Coworking Aggregators (Space-only, no groups)
| Platform | Model | Gap |
|----------|-------|-----|
| **myHQ (now FlexiPass)** | Aggregator for coworking space day passes | No grouping, no community, just space access |
| **GoFloaters** | On-demand workspace booking | Same — book a desk, no social layer |
| **CoFynd** | India's largest coworking aggregator (100K+ spaces) | Pure marketplace, no community |
| **Awfis** | Major operator (150+ centers, 88K seats) | Traditional coworking, no matching |
| **91springboard** | Community-focused coworking (27 hubs) | Closest to community, but still monthly memberships, no session-based groups |

### Key Insight
**No platform globally combines: (1) in-person, (2) small group matching, (3) at partner venues, (4) per-session pricing.** donedonadone is at a genuine intersection of whitespace.

## 2. HSR Layout Market

- **50-80 work-friendly cafes** in HSR Layout (Third Wave Coffee, Blue Tokai, Starbucks, Dialogues, Hole in the Wall, etc.)
- **15-25 dedicated coworking spaces** (JEEF, The Hive, Innov8, BHive, etc.)

- HSR Layout is one of the densest startup/tech neighborhoods in India

### Pricing Benchmarks
- Cafe spend: ₹300-500 per sitting (coffee + snack)
- Coworking day pass: ₹300-800/day
- Monthly coworking: ₹3,000-18,000/month
- Our pricing (₹299-799/session) sits right in the sweet spot

## 4. Jobs to Be Done

### Functional Jobs
- F1: Complete focused deep work without home distractions
- F2: Find a reliable, comfortable place to work for a few hours
- F3: Have fast wifi and power outlets available
- F4: Book a workspace quickly without monthly commitment
- F5: Discover new cafes/spaces to work from
- F6: Plan my work week with structured sessions

### Emotional Jobs
- E1: **Combat loneliness of solo work** (HIGHEST PRIORITY — severity 5/5)
- E2: Feel productive and accomplished after a work session
- E3: Feel part of a community of ambitious people
- E4: Reduce anxiety about unproductive days
- E5: Feel motivated by working alongside others (body doubling)
- E6: Feel a sense of belonging and identity
- E7: Enjoy the ritual of going somewhere to work

### Social Jobs
- S1: **Meet like-minded professionals** (HIGHEST PRIORITY)
- S2: Build accountability partnerships
- S3: Expand professional network organically (not forced networking)
- S4: Find potential collaborators or co-founders
- S5: Have light social interaction without full social obligation
- S6: Belong to a tribe/community

## 5. Bain's Elements of Value

### Must-Have (launch with these)
- **Connects** — Connects people who would otherwise work alone
- **Saves Time** — No need to organize coworking sessions yourself
- **Reduces Effort** — One-click booking, auto-grouping, venue discovery
- **Organizes** — Structures your work week with sessions

### Should-Have (build into the experience)
- **Affiliation/Belonging** — Being part of the donedonadone community
- **Motivation** — Body doubling effect, accountability
- **Reduces Anxiety** — Know where you're working, who you're meeting
- **Fun/Entertainment** — The group reveal, meeting new people

### Nice-to-Have (future features)
- **Self-Actualization** — Track productivity growth over time
- **Badge Value** — "I'm a donedonadone regular"
- **Provides Hope** — Build towards something bigger

## 6. Community Building Strategy (0 → 1000)

### Phase 1: Manual & High-Touch (0-50 users)
- Personally curate first groups
- Attend sessions yourself
- WhatsApp-based coordination
- 1:1 onboarding calls with first 20 users
- Partner with 3-5 venues personally

### Phase 2: Tribe Formation (50-250)
- Regular users start forming natural cliques
- Introduce "regulars" recognition
- Community events (monthly mixer, demo day)
- Referral program: "Bring a friend, get a free session"

### Phase 3: Systemization (250-1000)
- Platform handles matching automatically
- User-generated sessions (community hosts)
- Multiple venues, multiple areas
- Content marketing: "A day in the life of a donedonadone session"
- Partnership with startup incubators, freelancer communities

### Retention Mechanics
1. **Streak tracking** — "You've coworked 5 weeks in a row!"
2. **Group chemistry** — Option to "rebook with same group"
3. **Favorite venues** — One-tap rebooking
4. **Weekly digest** — "3 sessions available near you this week"
5. **Social proof** — "42 people coworked in HSR today"

## 7. Pricing Strategy

### Recommended Model (for launch)
- **Per-session pricing** (as decided): ₹299-799 per session
- Platform takes: ₹100 (2hr) / ₹150 (4hr)
- Venue takes: their own set price
- Total to user: ₹299-799 depending on venue

### Future Expansion
- **Session packs**: Buy 5 get 1 free
- **Monthly subscription**: ₹999/mo for discounted sessions
- **Free trial**: First session free (acquisition)
- **Referral credit**: ₹100 credit for referring a friend

### Unit Economics (at 1000 bookings/day)
- Revenue per booking (platform cut): ₹100-150
- Daily platform revenue: ₹100K-150K
- Monthly platform revenue: ₹30-45L
- Key costs: Supabase (~$25/mo), Vercel (~$20/mo), operations
- **Very healthy unit economics** at scale
